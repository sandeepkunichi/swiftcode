About SwiftCode

The requirement for highly reactive and scalable applications is now more than ever. New application frameworks and database design philosophies have helped application engineers to propose end-to-end solutions that scale to thousands of users. SwiftCode is a concentrated amount of engineering brought together by technology enthusiasts who have had experience in building and maintaining software for various use cases.

Our program is intended to show a modern age computer science graduate how to build scalable and robust applications using state of the art techniques and design methodologies that have shaped software today.

FAQ

  • Whom is the training program designed for?

    This training program is designed for IT students and aspirants who are passionate about programming. This program would give students a hands-on learning experience with latest technologies which are not only popular but are currently used to develop enterprise applications.

  • I am a student and I am not very sure if I am eligible for pursuing this program.

    We understand your dilemma very well. Of course you are eligible for this course if you posses following qualifications:

    • Pursuing B.E/ B Tech (Computer Science, Information Science and other related courses)
    • Prior exposure or basic knowledge in programming (C, C++, Java, Python etc)

  • Do you teach basics of programming?

    No.

  • I am interested in pursuing a career in Software Testing or Database Management. Can I enroll in this program ?

    No. This is only for aspiring application/systems developers.

  • Do you provide all the devices and infrastructure required for the training, or am I supposed to arrange them for myself ?

    We arrange all the required infrastructure.

  • How many students will be part of this program ?

    The seats are limited (≤25).

  • What is the class duration ?

    20-24 hours, spread across 3 days.

  • What else will I learn from this training program ?

    We provide insight into other aspects of software development like Source Control and Deployments.

  • Is a certificate issued after the completion of course ?

    Yes.

  • Will I get a practical exposure during the training ?

    Yes. It is designed to be a hands-on program.

  • What is scope of job placements ? Will I be offered a job by BETSOL ?

    his program is to train you to learn cutting-edge technologies and make you ready for the IT industry. Your chances for job placement will improve if you are better skilled; not only with BETSOL, but other companies as well.

  • What is the fee for training courses ?

    ₹ 2500.

  • Is the fee refundable?

    Fee once paid is not refundable.

  • How are the students selected for this program ?

    We perform a basic MCQ skill test for the students. The test will be conducted to see who is eligible for training. We check out your resumes as well.
